Research interests

I work in the areas of discrete, combinatorial and convex geometry. I enjoy thinking about lattice polytopes and convex polytopes, and triangulations and subdivisions of these, as well as the combinatorics and topology of simplicial complexes. My research has focused on covering minima of lattice polytopes, on unimodular covers and triangulations, and on algebraic and topological invariants of simplicial complexes.
